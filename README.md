## üöÄ Key Features
* **Dynamic Slicers:** Filter data by **Year (2015-2018)**, **Month**, **Shipping Class**, and **Region**.

* **Geographical Mapping:** Heatmap showing customer density by state (California leads with 2001 customers).
* **Profitability Analysis:** Detailed breakdown of sub-categories to identify loss-making products.

## üìà Executive Summary (Insights)
Based on the dashboard analysis:
1.  **Top Performer:** The **Technology** category is the most profitable, contributing **51%** of the total profit.
2.  **Geographic Strength:** The **West Region** (specifically California) represents the largest customer base.
3.  **Critical Issue:** While Sales are high for **Tables**, they are actually generating a **significant loss**, indicating a need for pricing or discount strategy review.
4.  **Operational Efficiency:** The overall profit margin stands at **12%** with an average discount of **16%**.
